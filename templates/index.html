About Us

Expertly curated abstract & citation database

Rapid growth of fake and predatory journals has seriously threatened the academic community. Our aim is to build a search engine that will not only result in accurate and authenticated journals but also detect fake or cloned journals, benefitting the academic community tremendously.

What is our goal?

Research, Innovation and Society.

To promote quality research, academic integrity and publication ethics in universities.

To promote high quality publications in reputed journals that would help in achieving higher global ranks.

To develop an approach and methodology for identification of good quality journals.

To prevent publications in predatory/ dubious / sub-standard journals, which reflect adversely and tarnish the image of academia.

Need for ACCUJOURNAL

Expand, Deepen and Spread Knowledge

The credibility of research publications is extremely important because it represents the academic image of not just an individual, but of the institution and the entire nation.

The number of research articles published in reputed journals is one of the globally-accepted indicators considered for various academic purposes such as institutional ranking

The problem of predatory / dubious / sub-standard journals has become a cause of serious concern all over the world.

The percentage of research articles published in poor quality journals is reported to be high throughout the world.

Publications in dubious journals reflect adversely leading to long-term academic damage and a tarnished image of an individual, institution and the nation.

Indexed journals are considered to be of higher quality in comparison to any non-indexed journals. Indexed journals enjoy better visibility and accessibility.
